description: "Wopee.io agents now test canvas-rendered apps and games: visual-coordinate clicks, configurable key-hold durations, and automatic iframe locator resolution."
---

Wopee.io agents can now test canvas-rendered apps and games. Clicks can target visual coordinates, and key presses support configurable hold durations for game controls. Locators inside iframes — including cross-origin iframes such as embedded payment or wallet flows — now resolve automatically, with no manual frame-switching in your test steps.
