Wopee.io agents can now run on your own LLM account. Configure Azure OpenAI, Google Vertex AI, Anthropic, or OpenAI per project — including OpenAI-compatible endpoints for internal gateways and proxies. Credentials are stored encrypted and resolved at runtime. For teams with data-residency or model-governance requirements, this means the agents use the models you already approved.
